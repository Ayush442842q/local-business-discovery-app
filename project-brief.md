# Project Brief: Local Business Discovery App

## Overview
The Local Business Discovery App is a web application designed to connect users with local businesses in their area, allowing them to discover and explore nearby shops, restaurants, and services. This platform aims to promote the trend of shopping local and supporting small businesses, providing users with a unique and authentic experience. By leveraging the power of online discovery, the app will help bridge the gap between local businesses and their target audience, fostering economic growth and community development. The app will provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to find and engage with local businesses.

## Target Users
The primary target users of the Local Business Discovery App are consumers in emerging markets, particularly millennials and Gen Z, who are interested in supporting local businesses and experiencing the unique culture of their community. These users are likely to be tech-savvy, active on social media, and eager to explore new and authentic experiences. By catering to this demographic, the app will tap into the growing demand for local and unique products and services, providing a valuable platform for both users and businesses.

## Core Features
- Feature 1: Business Listing and Search - a comprehensive directory of local businesses, allowing users to search and filter results based on categories, locations, and keywords.
- Feature 2: User Reviews and Ratings - a review system that enables users to share their experiences and rate local businesses, providing valuable feedback and social proof.
- Feature 3: Map-based Discovery - an integrated map view that allows users to visually discover local businesses, using the Google Maps API to provide accurate and up-to-date location data.
- Feature 4: Personalized Recommendations - an algorithm-driven recommendation system that suggests local businesses to users based on their search history, ratings, and preferences.
- Feature 5: Special Offers and Promotions - a feature that enables local businesses to create and promote special offers, discounts, and events, attracting users and driving sales.

## Tech Suggestions
We recommend using a technology stack that includes React for the frontend, Node.js as the backend framework, and MongoDB as the NoSQL database. The Google Maps API will be integrated for map-based discovery and location services. This stack offers a robust, scalable, and flexible foundation for the app, allowing for efficient development, deployment, and maintenance. React's component-based architecture will enable the development team to build reusable UI components, while Node.js will provide a fast and scalable backend framework. MongoDB's flexible schema will allow for easy adaptation to changing business requirements.

## Why Now
The trend of shopping local and supporting small businesses is on the rise, especially in emerging markets where consumers are looking for unique and authentic experiences. With the growing awareness of the importance of supporting local economies, now is the perfect time to build an application that facilitates this process. The lack of online discovery platforms in these regions presents a significant opportunity for the Local Business Discovery App to fill this gap, providing a valuable service to both users and businesses. By launching the app now, we can capitalize on this trend and establish a strong presence in the market.

## Success Criteria
The success of the Local Business Discovery App will be measured by the following key performance indicators (KPIs):
- User acquisition: 10,000 registered users within the first six months
- Business listings: 1,000 local businesses listed on the platform within the first year
- User engagement: an average of 500 user reviews and ratings per month
- Retention rate: 75% of users return to the app within a month of their initial visit
- Revenue growth: $10,000 in revenue generated through premium listings and advertising within the first year
By achieving these KPIs, the app will demonstrate its value proposition to users and businesses, establishing a strong foundation for future growth and expansion.
